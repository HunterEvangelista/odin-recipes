Protein Yogurt

Low Cal Meets High Protein

One of the things I am passionate about is working out and trying to build muscle. The best weapon to be successful in this endeavor is to have a wide range of healthy meals that are low calorie, filling, and have a high protein content. It helps that the healthy gut bacteria in greek yogurt helps with digestion too.

You can take this recipe as is or remove the granola and freeze it with other add-ins to create greek yogurt cookies. This recipe is simple and can be used as a quick breakfast or a night time snack to keep you full

Ingredients
  • 170g of fat-free unflavored greek yogurt. (I prefer Chobani)
  • 1 scoop of protein powder, I use Vanilla On. Anything that mixes in will work!
  • 1tsp of Cinnanom
  • Granola
Directions
  1. Mix together greek yogurt, protein powder, and cinnamon. It wil look like too much at first but it'll combine.
  2. Top with a little bit of granola for texture and enjoy your easy and quick healthy snack. This yogurt has nearly 50g of protein and only 300 calories.
