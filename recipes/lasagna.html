The Perfect Blue Collar Lasagna

This is my favorite lasagna that my wife makes me when I get back from a long day working in the steel mill. When I feel depressed from the long winters or low pay I reflect on how this recipe always makes me feel better. Starting with the perfectly cooked noodles that build the foundation for the meal and ending with a generous topping of heart healthy cheese, I can eat this every week!

The ingredients are simple and easy to get; however, the beauty of this recipe is it allows for some leeway with the ingredients that you choose! You can find everything you need at your local Wal-Mart.

Ingredients
  • 1 packet of lasagna noodles
  • 1 jar of red sauce
  • 1 whole yellow onion
  • 3 cloves of garlic
  • 3oz of mozerella
  • 3oz of anyother cheese of your choice!
  • OPTIONAL: 0.5lbs of lean ground beef for extra protein
Directions
  1. Cook the lasagna noodles per the manufacturer's directions. Your oven should be preheating to 400 during this time.
  2. Cook your lean ground beef in a skillet. Once cooked drain and rinse. Season with salt and pepper.
  3. Ladle a small amount of red sauce on the bottom of your lasagna dish.
  4. Once noodles have finished cooking shingle a single layer of your noodles.
  5. Add a layer of your meat and extra cheese to form the first layer. Repeat until all noodles, cheese, and meat is used.
  6. On your final layer add your mozerella cheese for a gooey topping!
  7. Cover your lasagna with foil and bake for 30 min. Uncover and bake for an additional 20 min.
  8. Rest for 20 minutes and enjoy!
